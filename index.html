Interests

My interests stem from my love for space. I believe there is no better way to spend my time then learning about rockets and exoplanets. Just last month, I got Neil Degrasse Tyson's signature at his talk on The Search for Life in the Universe. The entire experience was amazing! Something else I am interested in is English. I believe English is a necessary skill for anyone looking to lead a successful career, so I make sure to read at least one book every month to prepare.

Hobbies

My hobbies are all outdoor focused. For example, when I had the chance to go biking last weekend, I said yes immediately. There is nothing like spending a weekend hiking and biking on the side of a San Diego mountain. Another thing I love doing outdoors is playing soccer with my friends. Even though I do not play competitively, it is still great just to slow down with school work and focus on getting the ball into the net every once in a while.

Favorites

I have many favorite things that I have picked out throughout my life, as sometimes there is nothing to do besides rank every movie I have ever watched. To start off, my favorite book is Louis Sachar's Holes because though it is short, it is one of the best examples of how to write in a way that pulls readers in, something I aspire to do in my own writing. Next up, my favorite movie is The Godfather I. Though the second movie is often praised as the ultimate, I still believe that the first movie is best as it brings out really complex emotions and ideas that no other movie can match. Lastly, my favorite sport is track and field. I first discovered track and field in highschool, but once I joined, I realized that the sport really brings out the best of us, which is one of the main reasons I have stayed with it.
